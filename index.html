Fall is the Best Season!

Fall is the best season for many reasons. For one, the weather is just right. Not too hot, not too cool.
It's the peerfect sweater weather! Another great thing about fall is all the leaves changing colour.
Whether you want to take some nice photos or just enjoy the the scenery, fall is a beautiful season.
