Summer is the Best

Summer is the best season because it's hot! Can work on a tan, go to the beach, take road trips, there seem to be a lot more activities (particularly outdoors) during the summertime!

If you get too hot in the summer, you can easily take off layers or jump into a pool or cold shower for relief. Summer is also a great time to go out for patio drinks!

Summer is also the time of year where most people take vacation from school or work, so there's more chances to see people you are close to.

Fall is the Best Season!

Fall is the best season for many reasons. For one, the weather is just right. Not too hot, not too cool.
It's the peerfect sweater weather! Another great thing about fall is all the leaves changing colour.
Whether you want to take some nice photos or just enjoy the the scenery, fall is a beautiful season.

Fall also houses one the greatest holidays: Halloween. A great time for spooks, thrills, fun costumes, and lots of candy! With all these wonderful things, fall just can't be topped.
